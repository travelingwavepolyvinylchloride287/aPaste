## ✨ What aPaste Does

aPaste gives you a simple way to manage clipboard content.

### Clipboard History
Every time you copy something, aPaste saves it. You can open the history and find older items without copying them again.

### Pinboards
Pin items you want to keep close. Use pinboards for:
- text you paste often
- links
- short notes
- email templates
- code snippets you reuse

### Instant Search
Search your clipboard history from the app. Type a word or part of a phrase, then pick the item you need.

### Menu Bar Access
aPaste lives in the menu bar. You do not need to keep a big window open. Open it when you need it, then close it.
